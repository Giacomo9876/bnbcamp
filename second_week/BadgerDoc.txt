The steps for publicing an shitcoin or a token with ERC-20 are:

1 Go to the wizard to OpenZeppelin at the link https://docs.openzeppelin.com/contracts/4.x/wizard

2 Choose the mint option

3 Compile it on Remix

4 Choose on Metamask the BSCTestnet

5 Take BnB from faucet https://testnet.bnbchain.org/faucet-smart

6 Go for deploy on Remix having as option Injected Metamask(remember no wei or gwei value)

7 Deploy

8 Go on BSCTesnet and see the block already created, and proceed to verify and publish the contracts at https://testnet.bscscan.com/verifyContract

9 Fill the form and if needed on Remix do The flatten option for the contract created(if the are imported other smart-con)

10 Publish the contract.
